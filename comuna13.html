Comuna 13 Graffiti Tour AND METROCABLE WALKING TOUR

This tour takes you to Comuna 13, a once-dangerous neighborhood that has been transformed into a vibrant and colorful community through the power of graffiti. You'll see murals and other works of art that tell the story of Comuna 13's past and present, and you'll learn about the community's efforts to rebuild itself. Learn about the history of Comuna 13, from its violent past to its current status as a thriving community. See some of the most impressive graffiti in Medellin, created by local artists. Take a ride on the Metro Cables and enjoy the stunning views of the city. Meet some of the friendly locals and learn about their lives in Comuna 13. The Comuna13 Graffiti Tour and Metro Cables Medellin is a unique and unforgettable experience. If you're looking for a way to see a different side of Medellin and learn about the city's transformation, this is the tour for you. Here are some tips for taking the tour: Wear comfortable shoes, as you'll be doing a lot of walking. Bring sunscreen and a hat, as the weather in Medellin can be hot and sunny.
