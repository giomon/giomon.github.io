DOWN TOWN WALKING TOUR

Welcome to Medellin! This vibrant city is known for its friendly people, delicious food, and stunning scenery.

On this tour, you'll explore the heart of Medellin, from the historic center to the modern skyscrapers.

We'll start our tour at the Plaza Botero, home to a collection of 23 sculptures by the famous Colombian artist Fernando Botero.

From there, we'll walk through the pedestrian-only Carabobo Street, lined with shops and restaurants.

Next, we'll visit the Metropolitan Cathedral, a beautiful example of Gothic Revival architecture. We'll then take a ride on the Metrocable, a cable car system that offers stunning views of the city.

Our final stop will be at the Pueblito Paisa, a replica of a traditional Colombian village. Here, you can learn about Colombian culture and enjoy a delicious lunch.

This tour is a great way to get to know Medellin and experience all that this city has to offer. So what are you waiting for? Book your tour today!
